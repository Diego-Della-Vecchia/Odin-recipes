Stovetop Burger

Description

A hamburger, or simply burger, is a food consisting of fillings, usually a patty of ground meat, typically beef placed inside a sliced bun or bread roll

What you'll need:

  • Ground chuck
  • Your preffered seasoning
  • Cheese
  • Hamburger buns
  • Your prefered toppings
  • Your prefered condiments

Step 1

Mix together the seasoning and beef and roll form a beef patty

Step 2

Cook your beef pattys on the stove for 3-6min on each side.

Step 3

Assemble your burger
